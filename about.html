Our Story

A Legacy of Italian Excellence

Ostuni was born from a passion for authentic Italian cuisine and a desire to bring the warmth of Italian hospitality to Putney. Named after the beautiful white city in Puglia, our restaurant embodies the same timeless elegance and charm.

For over a decade, we've been serving traditional recipes passed down through generations, using only the finest ingredients sourced from Italy and local artisan producers. Every dish tells a story of heritage, craftsmanship, and love.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the plate. From our carefully curated wine list to our attentive service, we strive to create memorable experiences that transport you to the heart of Italy.
